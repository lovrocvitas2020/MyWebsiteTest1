sP1.2 – Identify, Prioritize and Aggregate Supply Chain Resources

This process focuses on understanding the supply chain’s available resources—such as facilities, labor, equipment, and technology—and aligning them with strategic goals. It ensures that resource planning supports demand fulfillment while optimizing cost, capacity, and responsiveness.

Key Activities

  • 🏭 Identify current and future resource capabilities across the network
  • 📌 Prioritize resources based on strategic importance and constraints
  • 📊 Aggregate resource data across business units and geographies
  • 🔄 Align resource planning with demand forecasts and supply chain strategy
  • 🧠 Evaluate trade-offs between cost, capacity, and flexibility

Outcomes

  • ✅ Clear visibility into supply chain resource availability and gaps
  • 📈 Improved resource utilization and investment planning
  • ⚙️ Enhanced agility and preparedness for demand fluctuations
