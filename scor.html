Overview

The SCOR (Supply Chain Operations Reference) framework traditionally groups processes into the following categories:

1) Plan

Aligning demand and capacity, inventory planning, sales and operations planning (S&OP), performance goals.

2) Source

Supplier selection, ordering, receiving, input quality control, management of raw materials and component inventory.

3) Make

Production, assembly, packaging, maintenance, quality control of finished products.

4) Deliver

Order processing, warehousing, shipping, transportation, customer delivery and billing.

5) Return

Returns from customers and to suppliers, complaint handling, repairs, recycling and disposal.

6) Enable

Supporting activities: data management, IT, compliance, risk management, human resources and supply chain finance.
