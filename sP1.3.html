sP1.3 – Balance Supply Chain Resources with Supply Chain Requirements

This process ensures that supply chain resources are effectively matched to the identified requirements. It involves evaluating trade-offs, resolving gaps, and making strategic decisions to optimize performance across cost, service, and risk dimensions. The goal is to create a feasible and aligned supply chain plan.

Key Activities

  • ⚖️ Compare aggregated requirements with available resources
  • 🔍 Identify gaps, constraints, and potential bottlenecks
  • 📈 Evaluate trade-offs between cost, service level, and flexibility
  • 🧩 Adjust plans to resolve imbalances and align with business goals
  • 🤝 Collaborate across functions to validate and approve the balanced plan

Outcomes

  • ✅ A synchronized supply chain plan that reflects both demand and capacity
  • 📊 Improved decision-making and resource allocation
  • 🚀 Enhanced responsiveness and operational efficiency
